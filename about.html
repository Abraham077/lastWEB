About Astana

Astana, the capital city of Kazakhstan, is a vibrant metropolis that seamlessly blends modernity with tradition. Formerly known as Akmola and Tselinograd, Astana was renamed in 1997 when it became the nation's capital.

The city is renowned for its futuristic architecture, including the iconic Bayterek Tower, Khan Shatyr Entertainment Center, and the Palace of Peace and Reconciliation. These landmarks make Astana a unique destination that attracts millions of visitors each year.

Our guide aims to showcase the rich cultural heritage, exciting events, and diverse activities that Astana offers to residents and tourists alike.

Our Mission

To provide residents and visitors with up-to-date information about events, activities, and attractions in Astana, making it easier to discover and enjoy all that our city has to offer.
